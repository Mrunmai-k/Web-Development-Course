The Best Movies According to Mrunmai

My top 3 Movies of all-time

Fight Club

The concept of revolting against the system is always challenging and also very intriguing. Fincher makes the film look effortless; shot like a fancy music video compilation and working like a magician's best trick. This will always be one of the most memorable, quotable and re-watchable movies of all time.

Once Upon a Time in the West

It says a lot that this film is chosen instead of the other Leone masterpiece listed below; but for all of its lengthy shots, staring characters and blue eyed villains, there's a charm to this film which makes everybody who stayed for the whole ride leave the cinema with a smile on their face and a simple "That was awesome!" on their lips.

Pulp Fiction (1994)

Not to be confused with unreliable narrator, this is THE film that basically started the fire of such movies. Every minute there's a shot or a dialog you will remember weeks after you first see the movie. Sam L. Jackson will never be the same in your eyes either.
